About Us

Learn more about the people that run Psych Solutions!

Howard Fleischman Ph.D. – President

Dr. Fleischman completed his undergraduate studies at Boston University followed by a Masters Degree in clinical psychology at Antioch College. Dr. Fleischman earned his Ph.D. from California School of Professional Psychology. Dr. Fleischman has over 30 years combined experience as a program and clinical director with California Pacific Medical Center in San Francisco & as a vice president & president of behavioral healthcare staffing companies with regional and statewide focus. During these 30 years as a manager, Dr. Fleischman has always maintained dual responsibilities as a direct service provider and manager. He has worked in a full continuum of service facilities including,outpatient, residential, forensic and inpatient settings.

Dr. Fleischman has extensive management experience in:

  • Program design and implementation
  • Team Building
  • Clinical supervision
  • Quality assurance
  • Peer review
  • Recruitment and retention

Dr. Fleischman has clinical experience and expertise in:

  • Anger management
  • Destructive/violent behavior
  • Crisis intervention
  • PTSD
  • Substance abuse
  • Individual/group therapy
  • Readjustment counseling

Rebecca Fleischman - Vice President

Rebecca Fleischman started her career as a 9-1-1 dispatcher for a small northern California city. She then moved to the California Department of Corrections and Rehabilitation (CDCR) and promoted through the ranks to an analyst’s position with the CDCR’s Health Care Services Department. She worked for the Health Care Services for seven years working in mental health contracts and medical appeals. During her time with the CDC&R, Rebecca gained a unique skill set working in a complex correctional system and a challenging inmate population.

Dr. Fleischman and Rebecca started Psych Solutions Incorporated in 2007. In her role as Vice President, Psych Solutions Inc. utilizes Rebecca’s tremendous organizational and analytical skills to oversee budget development, financial planning and billing/collections adjudication. Rebecca consults with Dr. Fleishman with all technical parts of contract development as well as bidding on new projects.

Raina Casaurang - Business and Operations Manager

Raina has been working with Psych Solutions for over 7 years. She is responsible for all aspects of invoicing and payroll. Raina is also responsible for recruiting and credentialing our independent contractors. Raina works closely with our contractors to ensure they get the support and resources they need to provide quality services. Raina is in close communication with managers and supervisors of our contracted facilities to maintain essential relationships with our customers. Psych Solutions Inc is priviledged to have Raina with our company.
